Here are the steps to install a useful Atom package called 'autoclose-html':

1.  Open Atom
2.  In the menu at the top of your screen, click on 'Atom' and then click 'Preferences'
3.  Once the Settings tab is open, click on 'Install' in the left menu.
4.  In the search bar, look up autoclose-html.  Install that package.
5.  Now click on 'Editor' in the left menu.
6.  Scroll towards the bottom and make sure 'Indent Guide' is checked.
